# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
AgroOS is a small team project built under hackathon conditions. We want this to be a respectful, welcoming space for everyone who contributes, regardless of experience level, background, or role on the team.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive in code reviews, issues, and discussions
- Assume good intent, especially under deadline pressure
- Give specific, actionable feedback rather than vague criticism
- Credit teammates' contributions fairly

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language or jokes
- Dismissing or belittling someone else's contributions
- Publishing someone else's private information without consent
- Deliberately introducing insecure code, secrets, or sabotaging shared work

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, commit messages, and any team communication related to AgroOS.

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to a project maintainer (see CONTRIBUTING.md once added, or contact the repo owner directly). Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributors who violate this Code of Conduct, at their discretion.

## Attribution
This is a condensed version inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/). For a fuller, more formal policy (e.g. if this project grows beyond the hackathon), consider adopting the full Contributor Covenant v2.1.
